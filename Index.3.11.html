What We Do

Chemical labeling is a crucial piece in communicating hazards associated with chemicals. It is the first key step in the process of using chemicals safely. Under the GHS, there are some new labeling requirements.

As defined in the GHS, are an appropriate group of written, printed or graphic informational elements concerning a hazardous chemical that are affixed to, printed on, or attached to the immediate container of a hazardous chemical, or to the outside packaging. The GHS requires chemical manufacturers, importers, or distributors to ensure that each container of hazardous chemicals leaving the workplace is labeled, tagged or marked with the following information: product identifier; signal word; hazard statement; precautionary statement; and pictogram; and name, address of the chemical manufacturer, importer, or other responsible part.
